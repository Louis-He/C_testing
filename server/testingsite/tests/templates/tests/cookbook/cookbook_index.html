Who are we?

We are some elfs from University of Toronto that want to make your life easier learning the programming language C/C++ and feel better when it is your first time entering the world of programming.

Who should read this?

The booklet is designed to be understandable even if you are a newbie to the programming world. The cookbook can also be used as a review material for the following courses. The cookbook includes most or all of the materials that an entry-level undergraduate C/C++ programming language course may cover.

  • ECE150 from University of Waterloo
  • APS105 from University of Toronto
  • ECE244 from University of Toronto
